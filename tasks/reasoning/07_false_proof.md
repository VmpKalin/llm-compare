The following "proof" claims that 2 = 1. Find the exact step where it goes wrong and explain why it is invalid.

Let a = b.
Multiply both sides by a:      a^2 = ab
Subtract b^2:                   a^2 - b^2 = ab - b^2
Factor:                         (a+b)(a-b) = b(a-b)
Divide both sides by (a-b):     a + b = b
Since a = b:                    2b = b
Divide by b:                    2 = 1
