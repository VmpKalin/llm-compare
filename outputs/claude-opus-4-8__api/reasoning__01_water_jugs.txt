# Measuring Exactly 4 Litres

1. Fill the 5-litre jug completely (5L jug: 5, 3L jug: 0)
2. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until it's full (5L jug: 2, 3L jug: 3)
3. Empty the 3-litre jug (5L jug: 2, 3L jug: 0)
4. Pour the remaining 2 litres from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug (5L jug: 0, 3L jug: 2)
5. Fill the 5-litre jug completely again (5L jug: 5, 3L jug: 2)
6. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until it's full (5L jug: 4, 3L jug: 3)

**Result:** The 5-litre jug now contains exactly 4 litres.
