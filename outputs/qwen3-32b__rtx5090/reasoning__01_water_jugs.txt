1. Fill the 5-litre jug to the top.  
2. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until the 3-litre jug is full.  
3. Empty the 3-litre jug.  
4. Pour the remaining 2 litres from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug.  
5. Fill the 5-litre jug to the top again.  
6. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until the 3-litre jug is full (this will require 1 litre).  
7. The 5-litre jug now contains exactly 4 litres.
