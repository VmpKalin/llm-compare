To solve the problem, we begin by defining the unknowns clearly.

Let the cost of the **ball** be $ x $ euros.  
Since the **bat costs 1 euro more than the ball**, the cost of the bat is $ x + 1 $ euros.  
The total cost of the bat and the ball together is given as **1.10 euros**.

---

### Step 1: Set up the equation

$$
x + (x + 1) = 1.10
$$

Simplify the left-hand side:

$$
2x + 1 = 1.10
$$

---

### Step 2: Solve for $ x $

Subtract 1 from both sides:

$$
2x = 1.10 - 1 = 0.10
$$

Now divide both sides by 2:

$$
x = \frac{0.10}{2} = 0.05
$$

---

### Step 3: Interpret the result

- The **ball costs** $ x = 0.05 $ euros (i.e., **5 cents**).
- The **bat costs** $ x + 1 = 0.05 + 1 = 1.05 $ euros.
- Total cost: $ 0.05 + 1.05 = 1.10 $ euros, which matches the given total.

---

### Final Answer:

$$
\boxed{0.05}
$$
