The bat and ball together cost €1.10, with the bat costing €1.00 more than the ball. Let the ball's cost be **x** euros. Then the bat costs **x + 1.00** euros. The equation becomes:

$$
x + (x + 1.00) = 1.10
$$

Simplify:

$$
2x + 1.00 = 1.10
$$

Subtract 1.00 from both sides:

$$
2x = 0.10
$$

Divide by 2:

$$
x = 0.05
$$

**Answer:** The ball costs **€0.05** (5 cents).  
**Verification:**  
- Ball: €0.05  
- Bat: €0.05 + €1.00 = €1.05  
- Total: €0.05 + €1.05 = **€1.10** ✅
