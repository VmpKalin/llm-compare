# Measuring 4 Litres

1. Fill the 5-litre jug completely with water.
2. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until the 3-litre jug is full.
3. Empty the 3-litre jug completely.
4. Pour the remaining 2 litres from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug.
5. Fill the 5-litre jug completely with water again.
6. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until it is full (adding 1 litre, since it already contained 2 litres).
7. The 5-litre jug now contains exactly 4 litres.
