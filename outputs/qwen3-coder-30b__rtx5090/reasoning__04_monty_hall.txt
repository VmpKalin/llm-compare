You should switch doors. If you stay with door 1, your probability of winning remains 1/3 (the probability that you initially picked the car). If you switch to door 2, your probability of winning becomes 2/3, because switching wins whenever you initially picked a goat (which happened 2/3 of the time). The host's action of revealing a goat doesn't change the initial probabilities - it simply eliminates one wrong option, concentrating the 2/3 chance of being wrong into the remaining unopened door.
