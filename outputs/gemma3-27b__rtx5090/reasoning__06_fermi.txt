**3. Total Number of Pianos in Berlin:**

**5. Workload Per Tuner:**

*   Over a year, that’s roughly 20 tunings/week * 50 weeks = **1000 tunings per tuner per year**. (I'm subtracting about two weeks for vacation/sick days)

**6. Number of Piano Tuners:**

*   Total pianos needing tuning (37,000) / Tunings per tuner per year (1000) = **37 piano tuners.**

**Final Estimate:**

Based on these assumptions, I estimate there are around **37 piano tuners** working in Berlin.
