Here's how to solve this problem:

**1. Find the individual rates:**

*   Pipe A fills 1/6 of the tank per hour.
*   Pipe B fills 1/4 of the tank per hour.
*   Pipe C empties 1/12 of the tank per hour.

**2. Calculate the combined rate:**

When all three pipes are open, the net filling rate is:

(1/6) + (1/4) - (1/12) = (2/12) + (3/12) - (1/12) = 4/12 = 1/3 of the tank per hour.

**3. Determine the time to fill the tank:**

Since the combined rate is 1/3 of the tank per hour, it will take 3 hours to fill the entire tank.

**Answer:**

*   **As a fraction of hours:** 3 hours
*   **In hours and minutes:** 3 hours + 0 minutes
