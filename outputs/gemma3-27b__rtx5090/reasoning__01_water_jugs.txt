1. Fill the 5-litre jug completely.
2. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until the 3-litre jug is full.
3. Empty the 3-litre jug.
4. Pour the remaining 2 litres from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug.
5. Fill the 5-litre jug completely.
6. Carefully pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug (which already contains 2 litres) until the 3-litre jug is full.
7. The 5-litre jug will now contain exactly 4 litres of water.
