Here's how to solve this classic riddle:

**Let:**

*  x = the cost of the ball
* x + 1.00 = the cost of the bat

**Set up the equation:**

The total cost is 1.10 euros, so:
x + (x + 1.00) = 1.10

**Solve for x:**

1. Combine like terms: 2x + 1.00 = 1.10
2. Subtract 1.00 from both sides: 2x = 0.10
3. Divide both sides by 2: x = 0.05

**Answer:** The ball costs 0.05 euros (or 5 cents).

**Reasoning:**

The key is to recognize that the difference in price between the bat and ball is a fixed amount (€1.00). By setting up an algebraic equation, we can account for this difference and solve for the cost of the ball.
