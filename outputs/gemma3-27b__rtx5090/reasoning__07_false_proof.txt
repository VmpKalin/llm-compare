The error occurs in the step where we **divide both sides by (a - b)**.

Here's why it's invalid:

We started with the assumption that `a = b`.  This means that `a - b = 0`. Division by zero is undefined and illegal in mathematics. Dividing by (a-b) is essentially dividing by zero, which makes the entire step invalid and leads to the false conclusion.

Even though the algebraic manipulation *looks* correct at first glance, it's built on an operation that isn't allowed.  You can't divide by a quantity that equals zero.
