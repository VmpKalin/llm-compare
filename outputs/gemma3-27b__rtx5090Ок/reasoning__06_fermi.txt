**2. Pianos per Person:**

*   Total pianos: 3,700,000 / 100 = **37,000 pianos**

*   Total tunings per year: 37,000 pianos * 2 tunings/year = **74,000 tunings**

*   Tunings per tuner per year: 5 tunings/day * 5 days/week * 48 weeks/year = **1200 tunings/year**

**5. Number of Tuners:**

*   Total tunings needed / Tunings per tuner = Number of tuners
*   74,000 tunings / 1200 tunings/tuner = **approximately 62 tuners**.

Based on these estimates, I'd guess there are around **60-70 piano tuners** working in Berlin.
