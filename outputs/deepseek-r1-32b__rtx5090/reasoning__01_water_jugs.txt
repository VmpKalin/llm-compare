To measure exactly 4 liters using a 3-liter jug and a 5-liter jug, follow these steps:

1. Fill the 5-liter jug completely with water.
2. Pour water from the 5-liter jug into the 3-liter jug until the 3-liter jug is full. This leaves 2 liters in the 5-liter jug.
3. Empty the 3-liter jug.
4. Transfer the remaining 2 liters from the 5-liter jug into the now-empty 3-liter jug.
5. Refill the 5-liter jug completely with water again.
6. Pour water from the 5-liter jug into the 3-liter jug until it's full. Since the 3-liter jug already contains 2 liters, only 1 liter will be transferred, leaving exactly 4 liters in the 5-liter jug.

Now, the 5-liter jug contains exactly 4 liters of water.
