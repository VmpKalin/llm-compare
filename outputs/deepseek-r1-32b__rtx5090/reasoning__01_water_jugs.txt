To measure exactly 4 litres using a 3-litre jug and a 5-litre jug, follow these steps:

1. Fill the 5-litre jug completely.

2. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until the 3-litre jug is full. This leaves 2 litres in the 5-litre jug.

3. Empty the 3-litre jug.

4. Transfer the remaining 2 litres from the 5-litre jug into the now-empty 3-litre jug.

5. Refill the 5-litre jug completely.

6. Pour water from the 5-litre jug into the 3-litre jug until the 3-litre jug is full. Since the 3-litre jug already contains 2 litres, only 1 litre will be poured from the 5-litre jug, leaving exactly 4 litres in the 5-litre jug.

Now, the 5-litre jug contains exactly 4 litres of water.
