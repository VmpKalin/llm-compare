To estimate the number of piano tuners in Berlin, we can follow these steps:

1. **Population**: Berlin has approximately 3 million people.
   
2. **Pianos per person**: Assuming one piano for every 200 people, there are about 15,000 pianos in Berlin (3,000,000 / 200).

3. **Tuning frequency**: Each piano is tuned approximately twice a year.

4. **Total tunings needed annually**: 15,000 pianos * 2 tunings/year = 30,000 tunings per year.

5. **Workload of a piano tuner**: A tuner can handle about 400 tunings per year (working 200 days at 2 tunings/day).

6. **Number of tuners needed**: 30,000 tunings / 400 tunings/tuner ≈ 75 piano tuners.

**Final Estimate**: There are approximately 75 piano tuners in Berlin.
