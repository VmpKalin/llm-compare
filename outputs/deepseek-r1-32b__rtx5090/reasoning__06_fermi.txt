To estimate the number of piano tuners in Berlin, we can follow this structured approach:

1. **Population Estimate**: Berlin has approximately 3 million residents.

2. **Piano Ownership Rate**: Assuming one piano per 100 people, there are about 30,000 pianos in Berlin (3,000,000 / 100).

3. **Tuning Frequency**: Each piano is tuned once a year on average.

4. **Tuner Workload**: A tuner can handle approximately 200 tunings per year (considering working days and time per tuning).

5. **Calculation**: Dividing the total number of annual tunings by the workload per tuner gives us 30,000 / 200 = 150 piano tuners.

**Final Estimate**: There are approximately 150 piano tuners in Berlin.
